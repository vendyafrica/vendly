FAQ

Good questions.

Do buyers need to create an account?
No. Name, phone, address, pay. That's it. We removed every step that wasn't absolutely necessary ‚Äî because every extra field loses you a sale.
What if I don't have many followers yet?
Then every buyer you do reach matters more. Shopvendly makes sure none of them fall through the cracks. Start with 100 followers. Grow from there.
How does the creator referral work?
You generate a product link with a creator's tag. They share it. When someone buys through it, both dashboards show the sale. You set the commission. We track it automatically.
Is it really free to start?
Yes. Your first 10 completed orders cost you nothing. After that, we take 4% of each sale. No monthly subscription, no upfront cost. You only pay when you make money.
What about delivery?
Your buyer gives their address at checkout. You arrange delivery your way ‚Äî your rider, your boda, whatever works. We handle the order and the payment. You handle the last mile.
Can I use it for TikTok too?
Yes. The link works anywhere. Post it anywhere ‚Äî Instagram bio, TikTok caption, WhatsApp status. It always leads back to your Shopvendly store.
